<h2>The Blade: Butterfly Timo Boll ALC</h2>
<p>Based on the identical Koto-ALC construction as the Viscaria, the <a href="/product/blade/butterfly-timo-boll-alc">Timo Boll ALC</a> features a slightly different glue and handle weight distribution. It tends to feel marginally stiffer and sharper. It is optimized for close-to-the-table counter-looping and precise, powerful attacks where ball placement is paramount.</p>

<h2>The Rubber: Butterfly Tenergy 05</h2>
<p>For more than ten years, <a href="/product/rubber/butterfly-tenergy-05">Butterfly Tenergy 05</a> was the undisputed king of table tennis rubbers. Its Spring Sponge technology creates an incredibly high throw angle, allowing players to simply "brush" the ball heavily and rely on the sponge to catapult the ball over the net with huge spin.</p>

<h2>How it Plays Together</h2>
<p>The stiffness of the TB ALC perfectly balances the "bounciness" of Tenergy 05. The blade gives the player direct feedback and flat-hitting power, while the rubbers handle the spin generation and arc.
