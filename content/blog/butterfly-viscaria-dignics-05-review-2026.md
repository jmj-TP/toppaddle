<h2>The Blade: Butterfly Viscaria</h2>
<p>The <a href="/product/blade/butterfly-viscaria">Butterfly Viscaria</a> is the carbon blade by which all others are measured. Featuring an Outer-ALC (Arylate-Carbon) composition and a Koto outer ply, it provides a crisp, direct feeling while significantly enlarging the sweet spot.</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Speed:</strong> 92/100</li>
  <li><strong>Control:</strong> 75/100</li>
  <li><strong>Feel:</strong> Medium-Stiff but heavily communicative.</li>
</ul>

<h2>The Rubber: Butterfly Dignics 05</h2>
<p>As the successor to the legendary Tenergy series, <a href="/product/rubber/butterfly-dignics-05">Butterfly Dignics 05</a> utilizes Spring Sponge X technology to grip the plastic poly-ball longer, delivering insane amounts of spin without losing speed.</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Throw Angle:</strong> Very High (Clears the net easily)</li>
  <li><strong>Durability:</strong> Significantly improved over previous generations.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why They Work Together Perfectly</h2>
<p>The Viscaria's Koto outer ply is hard, providing an instantaneous rebound for blocks and smashes. However, this stiffness requires a grippy, high-throw rubber to safely execute topspin arc. This is where <a href="/product/rubber/butterfly-dignics-05">Dignics 05</a> shines. The rubber "catches" the ball precisely when the blade's ALC fiber kicks in.</p>
